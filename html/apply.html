Who Should Apply?

This program is for you if:

  • You have completed basic homeopathy education (BHMS, diploma, or equivalent)
  • You're an intern or early practitioner seeking to deepen perceptual skills
  • You feel overwhelmed by encyclopedic knowledge and want systematic training in presence
  • You're curious about integrating AI into your practice ethically
  • You can commit 10-15 hours/week for 9 months
  • You value being therapeutic regardless of finding perfect similimum

This program may NOT be for you if:

  • You're a complete beginner to homeopathy
  • You're looking for quick-fix remedy protocols
  • You're skeptical about the value of Focusing/presence work
  • You're not comfortable with technology or AI tools
  • You cannot commit to weekly live sessions
