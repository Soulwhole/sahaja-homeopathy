The 9-Month Journey

A systematic path from presence to practice-ready practitioner

The PRESENCE Method

A systematic framework for homeopathic practice grounded in embodied awareness. Each letter represents both a skill and a way of being with patients.

P

Pause: Ground in Presence

Before the consultation begins: Take three conscious breaths. Feel your feet on the ground. Notice your own inner state. Clear residue from previous encounters. This is not meditation—it's professional preparation. Just as a surgeon scrubs in, you cultivate unprejudiced awareness.

Skill trained: Somatic grounding, attention cultivation, presence as deliberate practice.

R

Resonate: Attune to Deeper Meaning

As the patient speaks: Listen beneath words to felt sense. When they say "I feel stuck," notice: where do you sense that stuckness in your own body? Resonance is not analyzing—it's allowing their experience to register in you non-verbally, as sensation and quality.

Skill trained: Felt-sense attunement, somatic empathy, distinguishing thought from sensing.

E

Explore: Compassionate Co-Traveling

In the unfolding dialogue: Use Focusing-informed questions that invite patients deeper into their own experience. "When you say 'heavy,' what is the quality of that heaviness?" Not interrogating—accompanying them to edges they haven't yet articulated.

Skill trained: Therapeutic questioning, holding space, depth facilitation.

S

Synthesize: See the Whole Pattern

After deep listening: Offer back what you're sensing—not interpretation, but reflection. "I'm noticing a pattern of... does that resonate?" Give the patient a "handle" for their own experience. This synthesis itself can be therapeutic.

Skill trained: Pattern recognition through felt sense, mirroring, validation.

E

Eliminate: Remove Obstacles

Before prescribing: Identify what blocks the vital force—environmental, emotional, relational, chemical. Address these first. Sometimes obstacle removal is the remedy. Always consider: what needs to clear before healing can occur?

Skill trained: Holistic case analysis, obstacle recognition, health preservation.

N

Nurture: Support with Precision

When selecting remedy: Here AI becomes your partner. Feed the felt-sense pattern into AI tools. Cross-reference against superclass frameworks. Choose remedy from integrated human-AI intelligence. Precision matters—but follows presence.

Skill trained: AI-assisted remedy selection, integrating intuition with data.

C

Continue: Fresh Presence at Follow-Ups

At each return visit: Meet the patient anew. Notice what's shifted. Resist the urge to confirm previous diagnosis. Stay present to current reality. Follow-ups are not progress reports—they're fresh encounters.

Skill trained: Longitudinal care, case management, beginner's mind.

E

Evolve: The Lifelong Learning Path

Throughout your practice: Regular peer supervision. Continued Focusing practice. Engage with emerging research. Presence is not a destination but an ever-deepening capacity. Your instrument requires ongoing tuning.

Skill trained: Reflective practice, humility, professional development.

Three Phases of Development

1

Months 1-3: Presence Foundation

  • Daily Focusing practice (guided audio)
  • Unprejudiced observation training
  • Supervised case-taking (process emphasis, not remedy)
  • Somatic awareness cultivation
  • Introduction to AI tools
Outcome: You can maintain presence during consultations, articulate felt sense, and use basic AI assistance. You understand that therapeutic process matters as much as remedy selection.
2

Months 4-6: Pattern Recognition

  • Superclass framework as sensing tools
  • Kingdom differentiation through felt experience
  • Miasmatic patterns as energetic layers
  • Advanced AI partnership for remedy precision
  • Complex case analysis
Outcome: You sense patterns emerging from felt-sense work, not encyclopedic knowledge. AI handles detailed pattern matching while you stay attuned to the human encounter.
3

Months 7-9: Integration & Launch

  • Full caseload management (15-20 patients)
  • Obstacle removal and health preservation
  • Follow-up mastery
  • Professional practice setup
  • Business and ethics
Outcome: You're practice-ready. Managing patients confidently with presence as foundation, AI as partner, and a lifetime access to our growing content library for continued learning.
