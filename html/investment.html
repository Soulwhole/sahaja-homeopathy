Common Questions

Do I need prior homeopathy training?

Yes. This program is designed for homeopathic interns and practitioners, not beginners. You should have completed basic homeopathy education.

What if I can't attend live sessions?

All live sessions are recorded and available within 24 hours. However, live attendance is strongly encouraged for maximum benefit.

Is this accredited?

You'll receive a Sahaja Homeopathy completion certificate. We're exploring formal accreditation pathways for future cohorts.

What happens after the 9 months?

You retain lifetime access to all content library materials, can participate in alumni events, and have ongoing community access for support.
